English Language Lessons for Adults

What we offer is simple. We can teach you the English language for whatever purpose you might need to learn it.

IELTS Exams (or equivalent) and University Students.

If you are planning to study abroad or the United Kingdom for any degree, or study in your own country at a Masters or PhD level, we can prepare you for the IELTS exams, or equivalent exams that cover the entry requirements.

In fact, we can do more than that if you wish: we can focus on the key skills that you will need to develop in order to be able to prepare assignments, communicate with your peers and actively attend lectures and workshops in the English language.

And, needless to say, we can help a lot if you are planning on studying in the UK.

CEFR level Certificates

We can guide you towards any certificate that follows the structure of the Common European Framework of Reference for languages.

If you want to have your knowledge of English language formally recognized in every European country, we can help you prepare for every exam that is recognized in Europe, organized by any university or certification body, including University of Cambridge and University of Michigan exams.

Basic to Advanced English

Even if you know the very basic of the English Language, we can help you improve it. We can teach you English starting from the very basics and take you all the way to being a proficient user of the language.

Conversational vs Formal English

Even if you are a proficient user, there might be a difference between knowing the language and being able to use it.

Speaking English in a friendly manner is not the same as sending an important business e-mail. Chatting about business over drinks is not the same as sending an important business report. And none of these two are the same as learning English for an exam. We can help you put your knowledge of English language to use.

Study Support

Being an adult student is difficult.

How can you organise your time effectively? How can you make the most of your day? How can you make time for studying and what is the secret to staying motivated? We can offer our support, advice and workshops to help you as much as we can.

Visiting London

If you are an individual or a group that wants to visit London to enhance your English language learning experience, we might be able to help.

Even though we are not a travel agency, we have some ideas on how you can organise your itinerary to make your trip as educational as possible.

Translations

At the moment, we can translate texts from English to Greek and Greek to English. Timing, Price and Availability may vary, so please contact us here for further information.
